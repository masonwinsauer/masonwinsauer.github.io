The blackness was absolute. She wondered if her eyes hadn't adjusted, but they never did. Even in her advanced years, Sandra had retained her senses well. Her training directed her movement more than her rationale. She immediately spun against the wall to her left, a mental map exploding from the darkness. Her position allowed her to avoid any thing that was now trapped in here with her. Though she hadn't seen anything enter the space behind her, there was a new, ominous sound - One that was felt, not heard.
