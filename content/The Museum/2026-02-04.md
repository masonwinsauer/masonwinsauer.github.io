In favor of a traditional map showing the locations from a top-down view, the map provided upon entry is one with many artistic liberties taken. The creator's rationale was, in effect, that maps are bogus. They, as much as any of the exhibits themselves were of subjective value only. Maps ground you. In a temple to emotional overwhelm, they can offer a way to parse what you see. However, he denied them even that luxury. There will be no escape valve here.
